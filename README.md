# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis:
This project was completed as part of my internship at InternSpark. The objective was to analyze sales performance across different regions, categories, products, and time periods to identify business trends and provide actionable recommendations.

# Analyze sales data to
Understand regional sales performance
Identify top and bottom-performing products
Analyze category-wise sales contribution
Discover monthly and yearly sales trends
Generate business insights and recommendations

# 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Jupyter Notebook

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bhanupratapbiswas/superstore-sales

# Key Findings
Total revenue exceeded $2.26 Million
Technology was the highest-performing category
West region generated the highest sales
November was the best-performing month
2018 was the highest-performing year
Average Order Value reached $459.48

# Business Recommendations
Increase investment in Technology products.
Improve marketing efforts in low-performing regions.
Prepare inventory ahead of peak sales seasons.
Optimize or discontinue low-performing products.
Replicate successful strategies from high-performing regions.
